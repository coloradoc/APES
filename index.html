American History

What is America?

America is the idea of independence, justice, liberty, freedom and opportunity. The pursuit of creating a more perfect society and country. America was colonized by European settlers, leading to a struggle for freedom from British rule. The United States would emerge as a nation, gaining freedom and expanding its territories. America is a story of people striving for liberty, pursuing opportunities, and shaping a diverse and vibrant nation.
